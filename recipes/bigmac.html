Big Mac Recipe

Description

If you have ever been to McDonalds anywhere around the world, you may have tried the authentic and tasty Big Mac. The Big Mac recipe include the two sesame seed buns, slide round bread for middle, cheese, shredded lettuce, two beef patties, pickles, minced onions and secret-sauce named Thousand Island dressing.

If you are unsure of the preservatives that McDonalds serves you or want to save money or time, why not make Big Mac at home?
