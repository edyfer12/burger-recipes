Buttermilk Fried Chicken Burger

Description

The buttermilk chicken is very crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside. Buttermilk is a popular ingredient for making southern-style fried chicken or tenders. This ingredient is beneficial that the coating becomes very sticky all the flour never falls off when chicken is dipped.

What makes fried-chicken burger very tasty? When you bite into the chicken, not only the breading is tasty, but the garlic powder, paprika and cayenne pepper make it so wonderful. Inside the burger, what makes it bolder in taste is the lettuce, milk buns and mayonaise

Ingredients

  • 10 Free Range Chicken Thighs
  • Mustard Powder
  • Flour
  • Onion Powder
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Dried Sage
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Powder
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Dried Basil
  • Dried Thyme
  • Oil
  • 10 Milk Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Mayonaise

Steps:

  1. Combine the chicken, salt, pepper, garlic, dried mustard, paprika, and sage together in a large bowl. Stir together to coat the chicken evenly.
  2. Pour in the buttermilk over the seasoned chicken and stir again until well coated.
  3. Refrigerate the chicken mixture in the marinade.
  4. Whisk the flour, baking powder, salt, garlic powder, paprika, basil, thyme, onion powder, and cayenne pepper together in a shallow dish.
  5. Transfer each piece of chicken from the marinade into the shallow dish one at a time. Ensure the chicken is coated completely.
  6. Fry the coated chicken in hot oil, being careful not to overcrowd the pan. Cook the chicken until it's entirely golden brown on all sides.
  7. Remove the fried chicken from the vegetable oil and transfer it to a wire rack to allow any excess oil to drain off it.
  8. Fry 10 milkbuns, top and bottom sides, until golden brown.
  9. Cut the lettuce and place it on the bottom bun. Place the fried chicken on top of the lettuce. Pour mayonaise on top of the fried chicken. FEEL FREE TO EAT IT!
